# EviDex
**A Production-Grade Explainable Agentic AI Platform for Audit Evidence Collection, Validation, and Decision Traceability**

---

## Overview

**EviDex** is an end-to-end audit support platform that leverages **agentic AI architecture** to automate and enhance audit evidence collection, validation, and reporting.

Traditional audit workflows involve manual evidence requests, fragmented document review, and repetitive validation processes. EviDex addresses these challenges by introducing a structured, explainable, and traceable AI-assisted workflow.

EviDex is designed as an **audit assistant**, not a replacement for auditors. It preserves **human oversight**, ensuring all AI-driven outputs remain transparent, reviewable, and controllable.

---

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Agentic AI Workflow Engine
EviDex uses a multi-stage pipeline:
1. Request Interpretation  
2. Evidence Retrieval  
3. Evidence Validation  
4. Conclusion Generation  

Each stage operates independently with full traceability.

---

### 2. Explainability & Traceability
- Step-by-step workflow logs  
- Agent reasoning visibility ("Ask Agent Why")  
- Evidence linking in summaries (doc1, file.pdf)  
- Full audit trail for compliance  

---

### 3. AI-Assisted Decision Support
- Sufficiency assessment (Sufficient / Insufficient)  
- Confidence scoring  
- Risk and gap identification  
- Structured recommendations  

---

### 4. Automated Outputs
- Draft audit reports (exportable as PDF)  
- Follow-up email drafts  
- Executive and quick summaries  

---

### 5. Human-in-the-Loop Governance
- Editable AI outputs  
- Approval workflow (Approve / Reject / Revise)  
- Version-controlled reports  
- Audit-compliant archival (read-only mode)  

---

### 6. Dashboard & Monitoring
- Real-time metrics (AI confidence, processing time)  
- Workflow progress tracking  
- Recent activity feed  
- Multi-project filtering  

---

### 7. Evidence Management
- Evidence upload & preview  
- Inline document viewing  
- Download tracking with audit logs
